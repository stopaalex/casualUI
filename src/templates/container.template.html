This is a container that has a grey border.

Containers are just divs given specific classes (which you can learn more about by hovering over the "i")
classes used for the container element:
  • 'cui-container'
  • 'border-grey'
The line above us here is a spacer. These spacers can be given colors or just left transparent.
classes used for the spacer element:
  • 'cui-spacer'
  • 'grey'
